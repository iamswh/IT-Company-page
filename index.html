Smart Omni-Channel
Digital Solutions for the Smart Enterprise

Streebo offers a whole catalog of Digital solutions (web and mobile apps) catering to various business functions within the enterprise such as Marketing, Sales, Production, Manufacturing or Delivery, Distribution (Direct or through Agent), Procurement, HR, IT and Communication across a variety of industries and domains. Streebo’s solutions are powered by Automation & Artificial Intelligence technology from leading technology providers like IBM, Red Hat, Google and Amazon.

Hence, Streebo’s solutions are unique as they are truly omni-channel, meaning they can be deployed as web applications and/or mobile apps. Further, by leveraging “Conversational Artificial Intelligence” technology, Streebo delivers apps that are “voice and chat” activated meaning they will work across social media channels such as Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Google Chat, WeChat, Telegram, Signal and even SMS. Streebo’s omni-channel digital solutions can respond to voice commands from the likes of Amazon Alexa, Google Home and can be integrated with company’s existing IVR system as well. Employee facing applications can be extended to Internal Enterprise Messaging Platforms such as Microsoft Teams, Slack, HCL Sametime among others. Streebo’s Omni-Channel solutions are pre-integrated with a range of enterprise backend systems such as SAP, ServiceNow, Workday, IBM AS/400 Systems, PeopleSoft, IBM Maximo, IBM Cognos among others. Customers can choose to buy our AI powered Digital solutions or subscribe to them for a fixed monthly fee.
